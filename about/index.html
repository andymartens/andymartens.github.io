About

Mar 13th, 2015 4:43 pm

My approach here is to write concisely about data projects. That means I’ll leave out all sorts of ideas that are worth thinking about. I will do my best, however, to provide links to code and and details about the methodology. An interested person can then dig deeper into the choices I’ve made, the potential problems with these choices, and alternative ways to interpret the results. If you have question, let me know.
